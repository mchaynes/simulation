Rules:

  • The farther the circle's color is from the background, the lower its score
  • When colliding with another circle that has a different color, the circle with the lower score shrinks while the other grows
  • Circles of the same color don't interact
  • If the difference between two colliding circles radii is larger than 50, and the larger circles score is higher, it can change the smaller circle's color to its own
  • If "converge" is turned on, the background will slowly change to the color that occupies the most area
  • If "random" is turned on, the background will randomly change its rgb values by +-10 per 50 updates, it doesn't pay any attention to whether or not converge is on
  • To change the background color yourself, turn off Random Colors & Converge
